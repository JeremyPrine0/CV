About Me:

I am a husband, father, Army Reserve Officer, and Contract Specialist.

I am an Army Reserve Officer. Currently, my unit is mobilized at the Charleston Military Seaport on Joint Base Charleston, SC. Under normal conditions, Charleston Military Seaport is the busiest strategic seaport on the Eastcoast. Additionally, however, the White House has designated Charleston as the port for all Presidentially Directed cargo for Ukrainian aid. All our operations are considered "high visibility" because of extreme oversight of the White House. I am the Officer in Charge (OIC) of the team of Soldiers who manage terminal operations. I am totally responsible and personally accountable for everything my team does or fails to do. My Soldiers get the credit for all successes and I am responsible for anything that should have happened. I have personally touched every piece of Presidentially Directed Ukrainian cargo that has embarked from the United States since November 2022. It is a good feeling to know my work here is helping patriots in Eastern Europe win their freedom.

In addition to our Ukrainian Aid operations, we are also responsible for US Army units deploying to destinations across the globe. We conduct logistical planning, port staging, port clearance, cargo documentation, and vessel operations for US Army units departing for Europe (EUCOM), Africa (AFRICOM), and the Middle East (CENTCOM). We regularly deploy Brigade Combat Teams consisting of thousands of vehicles and pieces of equipment. The amount of logistical planning that goes into moving massive Army units and all their equipment from a base in the United States to another part of the planet is enormous. We coordinate with rail carriers, line-haul operators, cargo vessel operators, and Unionized labor. The planning starts months in advance.

I support the Army's mission in a civilian copacity as well. I work as a Contract Specialist for the Mission Installation Contract Command (MICC) at Fort Carson, Colorado. I work in the construction division of Contract Command. My role specializes in cradle to grave contract management for construction projects on Fort Carson. I have managed several million dollars worth of construction contracts.
